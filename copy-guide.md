## Tone & Style Guidelines

- Keep the tone friendly, warm, and inviting.
- Use simple, natural language that sounds like a real local salon.
- Focus on customer outcomes: confidence, comfort, cleanliness, value, and event-readiness.
- Avoid stiff phrases, exaggerated claims, and generic filler.
- Keep the brand position clear: affordable luxury beauty care with professional results and a personal touch.
